in‧qui‧site

/ɪŋˈkwɪzət/
  1. (verb) (deprecated) from Latin inquīsītus, past particple of inquīrō: To make inquisition; inquire. (New Century 1911)
  2. (adjective) Exceedingly unrefined or lacking in polish; imperfect, incomplete, or pending revision.
  3. (verb) To correspond with intent to learn; to inquire electronically.
